**Full description:**
Gumiho RPG is a fast-paced top-down twin-stick shooter built for mobile.

- Move with the left joystick, aim and shoot with the right
- Fight zombies, monsters, and tanks across 10 challenging levels
- Earn coins, buy new guns and character skins in the shop
- Limited bombs for big area damage when you're surrounded
- Earn up to 3 stars per level based on your health at the end
- Play in English or Khmer

Survive the waves. Upgrade your gear. Become a legend.
